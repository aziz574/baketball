Kuai Deng

Kuai Deng is the best South Australian basketball prospect you haven't heard about. The 6’10 star dominates games with a relentless motor and competitive spirit, and will be keen to make a name for himself at LeBron James’ old alma mater The Patrick School across the 24-25 season.

  • Height: 6'10
  • Class: 2025
  • Club: Eastside Rams
  • From: Grange, SA
  • School: The Patrick School (NJ)
  • AUS High School: Trinity College

Kuai Deng has come a long way to end up at The Patrick School. The 6'10 big man comes from a family of five, supported by his mother, who worked three jobs on top of studying.

Combining unteachable athletic gifts with a relentless motor and passion for the game, Deng quickly emerged as a stand-out youth player. Representing the Eastside Rams, Deng became the program's star recruit.

Despite his talent, Deng never represented his state at a National Championship. His access to the prep school system has quickly facilitated both his development and his exposure, with high major Division 1 colleges already knocking at his door. The assessment from coaches? Deng has all the talent to be a pro at the games highest levels.
